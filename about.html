Who We Are

Started Small.
Grown Strong.

SwiftShip was founded in 2016 by Rajan Kumar, a logistics professional with over 12 years of experience in the courier industry. What started as a small office in Anna Nagar, Trichy with just 3 delivery agents has grown into a full-scale courier operation serving 100+ cities across India.

We built SwiftShip on one simple belief — every package carries something important to someone. Whether it's a business document, a gift for a loved one, or an e-commerce order, we treat every shipment with the same care and urgency.
